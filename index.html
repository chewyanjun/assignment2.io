My Journey


As a child I have always been into arts and crafts, especially origami. My first encounter with 3D origami was in

upper primary when I was fascinated by my friend's 3D origami penguin which she brought to school. After which, I began

watching many YouTube videos on 3D origami which led me to learn more about the world of 3D origami. I like to compare

3D origami with lego because both of them allow one to express their creativity by creating almost anything imaginable.

I started off with smaller projects such as penguins and swans before moving on to bigger projects such as wedding 

ornaments. The following images are wedding gifts that I made using 3D origami.
